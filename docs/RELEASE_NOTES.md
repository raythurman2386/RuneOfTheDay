# Release Notes

## 1.1.0 — App Store & Google Play

### App Store (iOS) release notes

> Bug fixes and a small notification fix. The daily rune you get in your
> morning notification now matches the rune you'll see when you open
> the app — no more "yesterday's rune" surprise.
>
> You can also pick when the daily rune unlocks: head to Settings to
> choose any time from 5:00 AM to 9:45 AM.
>
> Thanks for using Rune Of The Day!

### Google Play release notes

> Bug fixes and improvements:
> • Daily rune notification now matches today's rune
> • Choose your daily rune unlock time in Settings
> • Layout fixes for notched and edge-to-edge displays
>
> Thanks for using Rune Of The Day!

### Short blurb (if a single line is required)

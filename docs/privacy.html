Privacy Policy - Rune of the Day

Last updated: March 16, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Rune of the Day. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that your device may store while using our app. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data handling in our mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

Rune of the Day is designed with privacy in mind and operates entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about our users.

Local Storage

Our app uses local storage (AsyncStorage) on your device to:

  • Remember your daily rune selection
  • Store your theme preferences (light/dark mode)

This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security settings. We recommend keeping your device's operating system and our app updated to the latest version.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party services that collect information about you.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

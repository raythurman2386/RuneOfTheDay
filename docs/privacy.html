Privacy Policy

The Short Version

Rune of the Day collects no data. Period. The app works entirely offline. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no third-party trackers. Everything you do stays on your device.

Introduction

Welcome to Rune of the Day, developed by Ravenwood Creations. We believe privacy is a feature, not an afterthought. This Privacy Policy explains exactly what information the app does and does not handle so you can use it with confidence.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Rune of the Day has no servers, no databases, and no backend. The app never sends any data anywhere.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Information Stored On Your Device

The app uses your device's local storage (AsyncStorage) to remember your preferences and daily rune. This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and is not shared with any third party.

Specifically, the app stores:

  • Daily rune — the rune selected for the current day and its orientation (upright or reversed)
  • Theme preference — your choice of light, dark, or system theme
  • Haptics preference — whether haptic feedback is enabled
  • Notification time — your chosen daily reminder time (5:00 AM–9:45 AM)

You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app or through your device's system settings.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Rune of the Day schedules a local notification on your device. These notifications are handled entirely by your operating system — no data is sent to us. You can disable notifications at any time in the app's Settings screen or in your device's system settings.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions, each tied directly to a feature you control:

  • Notifications — used only if you opt in to daily reminders
  • Vibration (Android) — used for haptic feedback, which you can disable in Settings
  • Receive boot completed (Android) — allows scheduled notifications to persist after a device restart

No permission is required for core app functionality.

Third-Party Services

Rune of the Day does not use any third-party services that collect information about you. There are no analytics SDKs, ad networks, or tracking libraries included in the app.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for all ages.

Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's own security settings (passcode, biometric lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your operating system and the app updated to the latest version.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with an updated "Last updated" date. If we ever introduce a feature that changes how data is handled, we will update this policy accordingly before the change takes effect.
